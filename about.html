Our Story

Why we built Porter

We spent years watching businesses overpay for visitor management systems that should be simple. Companies were choosing between overpriced enterprise tools or cobbling together spreadsheets and paper sign-in books.

Porter was born from a simple question: why can't visitor management be powerful, affordable, and easy to set up? We set out to build the system we wished existed — one that includes every feature at a fair price.

Based in the UK, we understand the unique requirements of British businesses — from GDPR compliance to the practicalities of managing visitors across multiple sites. Porter is built with these needs at its core.

Today, Porter serves hundreds of locations across the UK, helping businesses welcome visitors professionally while staying compliant and secure.
